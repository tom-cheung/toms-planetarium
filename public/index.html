Welcome to Tom's Planetarium

Inspired by a clear star filled night sky, this project aims to show some of the beauty found in our solar system.

Instructions for interacting with this model of the solar system (not to scale... yet)

  • Using the scroll wheel on your mouse, you can zoom in and out of the solar system
  • By default the view is centered on the sun, by clicking any of the planet buttons on the top of the page you change the focus
  • Holding the left mouse button down and moving your mouse will rotate your view
  • To close these instructions click the X button found on the upper right hand corner, or click anywhere outside of this window
  • To reopen the instruction click the Instructions button found on the upper left hand corner of this page
  • I hope you enjoy this application!
